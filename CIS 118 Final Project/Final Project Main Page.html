My Project On EMINEM

By Samuel Arnold

Eminem (aka Marshall Bruce Mathers III) is an American rapper and record producer from Detroit Michigan. He is credited with popularizing hip hop in middle America and is critically acclaimed as one of the greatest rappers of all time. His global success and acclaimed works are widely regarded as having broken racial barriers for the acceptance of white rappers in popular music.
