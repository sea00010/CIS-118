Doodling

Did you know that you remember about 10% of what you read, but 90% of what you see? Using doodles for note taking can help to improve your memory, understanding, and help you solve problems, by combining the senses of sight, hearing, and touch!

Materials

You can choose to draw with whatever you have or feel most comfortable with, but a pencil (with an eraser) is a great choice to start out with.

Grab a sheet from your printer, a post-it, or get a sketchbook (carry around a Moleskin sketchbook in your pocket)!
